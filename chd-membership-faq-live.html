What is my username?

Your email address is your username.

What if I get an error saying my email address is not recognized?

This typically happens when members are using multiple email addresses. You must use the email address submitted when completing the membership form.

I recently became a member, but I didn't receive any access information. How do I get access?

Go to our member login page:
  • Select “Forgot/Reset Password.”
  • Enter your email address and select “Get New Password.”
  • You will receive an email with a confirmation link. If you do not see it in your inbox, check your spam folder.
  • Click on the link in the email to reset your password.

I still can't gain access. What can I do?

Please contact us for assistance.

How do I get to the Members-only section of the site?

The Members Only link is at the top of every page of the site.
